About Valar Express

Valar Express is an emerging industrial solutions company built on hands-on experience across manufacturing processes. Our roots are in metallurgy and heat treatment, and our work extends into grinding, cutting, laboratory systems, coolant management and industrial spares.

We support Indian industries with practical, reliable and scalable solutions. Our sourcing approach starts with Make-in-India partners and expands to trusted international suppliers when specialized requirements demand it.

Our portfolio includes furnace spare parts, lab filtration membranes, coolant-system centrifuges, laboratory apparatus and a growing range of industrial components shaped by customer needs.

Our Mission

To empower industries with dependable, cost-effective solutions and service-led support.

Our Vision

To become a trusted global spare parts and industrial solutions partner for Indian manufacturing growth.
